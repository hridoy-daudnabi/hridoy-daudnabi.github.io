About me

As a civil engineering graduate student specializing in transportation engineering, my expertise encompasses Transportation Demand Management (TDM), econometric modeling, survey design, and the application of machine learning to address complex transportation issues. With a fervent passion for sustainable and active modes of transportation, my work aims to promote environmentally friendly alternatives that enhance urban mobility and reduce carbon emissions. Additionally, my interest in air transportation aligns with my broader vision of creating more efficient and sustainable transportation networks globally. Through my academic research and professional projects, I am dedicated to innovating within the transportation sector, leveraging data-driven insights to optimize system performance and improve the quality of life in communities. My commitment to sustainability and active transportation is not just a professional stance but a personal one, reflecting my belief in the importance of developing eco-friendly and accessible transportation solutions for the future.
